TechnicalMashup Cart !

TechnicalMashup Cart Private Limited, is an Indian e-commerce company based in Odisha, India. It was founded by Bibhas Sahu in 2017. The company initially focused on Technology related things, before expanding into other shopping product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials & groceries, lifestyle products etc. The service competes primarily with different local shopping mall or shopping site, and the domestic rival local shop. As of March 2020, Technical Mashup Cart held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry. Technical Mashup Cart is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel (a position that was bolstered by its acquisitions of Myntra and Jabong.com), and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones. Technical Mashup Cart also owns PhonePe, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI).
